ERP

ERP/CRM Implementation

While every enterprise has knowledge of its resources and customer base, very few companies have consolidated a comprehensive enough database of information to achieve a complete view of its business processes. Optimization of this information - channeling, defining, analyzing and using it is the key to achieving organizational efficiency.

Successful implementation of any business system demands the adoption of a well-planned and methodological approach. Our professional consultants provide the services needed to manage and integrate your new business systems into the day-to-day operations of your company.

At Strategic IT Corp we have extensive experience implementing today's leading ERP and CRM packages.

Whether it's Microsoft CRM or Salesforce.com - we have the functional and technical expertise in-house to provide full life-cycle implementation and support services.

Microsoft Dynamics

From centralizing customer information and correspondence across your organization to identifying new avenues of business growth to capturing proven workflows, CRM has features and components that can help your business become more responsive to your customer's needs.

CRM products help organizations of all sizes, within all industries, to build more fruitful, productive relationships with current customers, and forge relationships with new ones.

Microsoft Dynamics CRM is an affordable technology solution that encompasses all the best practices of CRM, with all the flexibility, familiarity, and functionality you'd expect from Microsoft. It's a CRM solution that works the way you do, the way your business does, and the way technology should.

  • CRM for Sales fuels sales representative productivity and results.
  • CRM for Marketing helps transform information into knowledge to optimize your marketing mix.
  • CRM Customer Service helps your team transform customer interactions into meaningful customer relationships.
  • CRM Analytics helps you use your business' information to make better decisions.
  • CRM Workflow helps automate repetitive work tasks and streamline business processes.

Strategic IT Corp is a Microsoft Gold Certified Partner and has deep Microsoft CRM implementation, Microsoft CRM customization, and upgrade experience.
